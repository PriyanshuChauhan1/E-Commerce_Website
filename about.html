Who We Are?

Started in 2007,Zara has enabled millions of sellers, merchants, and small businesses to participate in India's digital commerce revolution. With a registered customer base of more than 450 million,Zara's marketplace offers over 150 million products across 80+ categories.With a focus on empowering and delighting every Indian by delivering value through technology and innovation, Zara has created lakhs of jobs in the ecosystem while empowering generations of entrepreneurs and MSMEs. Zara is known for pioneering services such as Cash on Delivery, No Cost EMI and easy returns, which are customer-centric innovations that have made online shopping more accessible and affordable for millions of Indians.
